What is it?

Supercharge placing workers comp insurance with CompMatrix. Estimates from every carrier in under 5 seconds. Detailed breakdowns of every quote help you find markets faster and spend more time on what's important. A community of workers comp professionals working together.

Who is it for?

Designed by insurance agents for insurance agents. We were tired of wasting time with constant declinations and it was only getting worse in a tight market. We created CompMatrix to cut through the noise and find carriers who will quote as fast as possible. 

Why you'll love it

Let's face it, comp can be frustrating. Let us keep track of carrier appetite and price trends. Stop submitting business to every carrier, find the cheapest and most likely to quote with CompMatrix. Discover new carriers through other agents and timely submissions a thing of the past. 

Find a market in seconds, it's that easy.

Class code, payroll, experience modifier and boom! Hundreds of estimates at your fingertips. Dive into more detail, adjust them to your specs, view underwriting appetite quirks for each company. Everything is right here. 

It's a community for workers comp agents only.

You shouldn't be going the road alone. Every agent understands their carriers the best. We let you adjust prices and provide tips about dealing with your underwriters. Then we compile everything and share it with the community. Best of all, this tool is just for agents. There's power in numbers.
